FAQ

  • Power: 120V (same as the U.S.).
  • Alcohol Hours: Not served/sold from 12:00 a.m. to 9:00 a.m.
  • Drinking Age: 18 (not strictly enforced).
  • Language: Many younger Tanitians speak English; limited in rural areas.
  • Healthcare: One hospital and several clinics; many multilingual staff.
  • Safety: Violent crime is rare; be mindful of pickpocketing.
  • Holidays: Many national holidays; attractions may close.
  • Currency: U.S. dollar; many accept euros and yen; credit cards widely accepted.
